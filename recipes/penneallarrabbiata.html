Penne all'Arrabbiata


Description

Penne all'Arrabbiata is a spicy Italian pasta dish that is made with a tomato-based sauce, red pepper flakes, and garlic. This classic dish is quick and easy to make and can be served as a main dish or as a side dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Penne pasta
  • 28 ounces canned diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the penne pasta according to the package directions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes to the skillet and saut√© for 1-2 minutes, or until the garlic is fragrant.
  4. Add the canned tomatoes to the skillet and stir to combine. Cook for 10-15 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened slightly.
  5. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Add the cooked penne to the skillet with the sauce and toss to coat evenly.
  7. Garnish with chopped parsley and serve hot.
