Ratatouille Recipe


Delicious and Healthy

Ratatouille is a classic French vegetable stew that's easy to make and packed with flavor. It's a great way to use up fresh summer produce and can be served as a main dish or a side.

Ingredients

  • 1 large eggplant, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 2 medium zucchini, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 yellow onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Heat the olive oil in a large oven-safe skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add the chopped onion and sauté until softened, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add the minced garlic and sauté for an additional minute.
  5. Add the eggplant, zucchini, and bell pepper to the skillet and stir to combine.
  6. Add the chopped tomatoes, thyme, basil, salt, and pepper to the skillet and stir to combine.
  7. Cover the skillet with a lid or foil and bake in the preheated oven for 30-40 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.
  8. Serve hot or cold, garnished with fresh herbs if desired.
