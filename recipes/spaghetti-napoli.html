Spaghetti Napoli


That's it

Spaghetti Napoli is a classic Italian dish that features a tangy tomato sauce with garlic, onions, and basil. This simple yet flavorful recipe is a favorite for its ease of preparation and delicious taste. Serve with freshly grated Parmesan cheese and crusty bread for a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound Spaghetti
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package directions until al dente.
  2. While the spaghetti is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add the diced onion and sauté for 5 minutes or until softened.
  4. Add the minced garlic and sauté for an additional minute.
  5. Add the crushed tomatoes and chopped basil to the skillet and stir to combine.
  6. Simmer the sauce for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens slightly.
  7. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Drain the cooked spaghetti and add it to the skillet with the sauce. Toss to coat the spaghetti evenly with the sauce.
  9. Serve hot, topped with freshly grated Parmesan cheese.
